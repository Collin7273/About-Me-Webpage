Interests

I am interested in a lot of things. Cars are one if not many. I am always interested in learning about new models and technologies. I enjoy going for drives and exploring places I have never been to. I am a big fan of music. I listen to rap, rock, country, and Japanese rock to name a few genres. I am also a big fan of Japanese animation or anime for short. I love the stories, characters, the underlying themes, and the art style that they use. My favorite anime of all time is the Fate series. It's about seven mages that summon seven servants for history, myths, and legends to fight in a free-for-all to win the Holy Grail.
I am fascinated by technology and how it's advancing. I enjoy learning about new technology and software. I also enjoy building and fixing things. I like taking things apart and figuring out how they work. I am also a big fan of video games, especially racing games. I love being able to drive my favorite cars and racing against other players. I also enjoy playing other genres such as action, adventure, and strategy games.
